About

About GIC

Gutierrez Inspections and Consulting, based in Utah, USA, is recognized for its expertise and commitment. With over 30 years of experience in both the private and public sectors, our team has a deep understanding of client needs. We are dedicated to identifying locations that precisely match our customers' requirements. Recently, we expanded our services to include specialized advice on property investments, home safety, and compliance with local building codes. This broadening of services enhances our ability to support clients in making informed decisions about their property investments and ongoing home maintenance.
